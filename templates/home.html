Visit the Ooty

Ooty earned the title "Queen of Hill Stations" due to its captivating landscapes, lush green valleys, and rolling hills. The town is surrounded by the Nilgiri Mountains, which are part of the Western Ghats, and is home to numerous tea gardens, dense forests, and beautiful lakes.

Thanjavur is famous for Siva Temple

Thanjavur (Tanjavur or Tanjore) is a temple site in the Tamil Nadu region of southern India. Thanjavur was the capital of the great Chola (Cola) king Rajaraja I, and it was he who commissioned the site's magnificent temple, the Brihadishvara, in the early 11th century CE.
